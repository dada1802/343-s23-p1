About us
David & Simon's Diner is a family owned diner based in Harrisonburg, Virginia. For the past couple of decades, our mission has been to bring the freshest and most convenient array of foods to all our customers! From our vegetables grown fresh from our gardens to our clean cultivated meats, we offer the highest quality foods at our diner and excellect customer service!
