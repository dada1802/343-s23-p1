Report

  1. We have a total of 7 html pages that all carry meaning in our project (not including report.html)
  2. All of our pages present a favicon at the top of each page.
  3. Most (if not all) of our pages have at least one header present.
  4. Some of our pages link to different aspects of our site displaying different kinds of food and generally to navigate around.
  5. All of our pages contain media queries for a resolution screen size for an iPhone 12 Pro, which is what we decided to use as a mobile version.
  6. Most of our pages consist of various images of all the food being served with their designated alt attributes.
  7. The entire site has been checked for errors and warnings with both the Validator and WAVE Web Accessibility Evaluator.
  8. Some of our pages utilize different fonts varying from 'Courier New'to 'Gill Sans.'
  9. Our home page has an "order" button that leads to an Order Form that has you put in your info whenever you want to order food online.
  10. Our report page has a link to our project sketch externally from our website.
  11. Our site can be found at github.
